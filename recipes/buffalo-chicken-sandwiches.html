Buffalo Chicken Sandwiches

Description

This crockpot Buffalo chicken is great for hearty sandwiches that will please those who love Buffalo chicken wings. This recipe is perfect for those days spent watching football.

This recipe requires 10 minutes to prepare and up to 6 hours of cook time resulting in 6 servings.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 bottle Buffalo wing sauce, divided
  • Dry ranch salad dressing mix
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts into the slow cooker; pour 3/4 of the wing sauce and ranch dressing mix
  2. Cover and cook on low for 6 to 7 hours.
  3. Shred chicken in the cooker with 2 forks. Stir in butter
  4. Pile shredded chicken and sauce onto hoagie rolls. Serve with remaning Buffalo sauce
