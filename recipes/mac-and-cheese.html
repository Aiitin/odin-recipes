Slow Cooked Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a recipe for the Slow Cooked Mac and Cheese, a creamy and comforting meal that only takes moments to assemble in a slow cooker. Great for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners!

This reciepe requires the use of a slow cooker, it will take 15 minutes to prepare and around 3 hours to cook resulting in 12 servings. This recipe is not vegan friendly.

Ingredients

  • 1 package elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt ans ground black pepper
  • 1 package shredded cheese
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 can condensed cheddat cheese soup
  • 1 pinch paprika (optional)

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water, stir macaroni and return to boil. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, unitl tender yet firm to the bite. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.
  2. Add butter to pasta and stir, season with salt and pepper. Sprinkle 1/2 of the cheese over pasta.
  3. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  4. Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smotth, stir into pasta mixture.
  5. Sprinkle remaning cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika.
  6. Cook on low for 3 hours.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
