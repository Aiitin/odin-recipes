Tuna Sandwich

Description

The Tuna Sandwich is an easy and delicious meal to make with many different possible variations to ensure you never get tired of it, this recipe is a basic edition of a tuna sandwich but many alterations can be made.

This reciepe takes 5 minutes to prepare, if cold tuna is preferred then refrigerate for at least 30 minutes. Results in 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 can of (any) skipjack tuna
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Lettuce
  • Cucumber
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt
  • Red chilli powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Open can of tuna and put into bowl and mix with fork.
  2. Put 2 spoons of mayonnaise in bowl and mix.
  3. Cut lettuce and cucumber and mix into bowl.
  4. Sprinkle salt and red chilli powder (optional).
  5. Put in fridge (if wanted cold).
  6. Toast bread.
  7. Spread tuna onto bread.
